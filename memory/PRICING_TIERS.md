# 💰 InnFeel Pricing Tiers — LOCKED 2026-06

> Decisions finalised based on cost analysis + competitive benchmarking.
> Source: chat session 2026-06 with founder.

## 🆓 Free
- 1 lifetime coaching trial
- 7 days of mood history
- Up to 25 friends
- Standard auras (no audio/video/music/text descriptions)
- 1 daily push notification at noon local

## 🟣 Pro — €6,99/month
- **10 coachings per day**
- Unlimited mood history
- Unlimited friends
- Audio + video + music + text auras
- Close friends list
- Detailed analytics & weekly recap email
- Streak freeze (1/month) — protects your streak if you miss a day
- Custom emotion intensities (>5)

## 🌟 Zen — €14,99/month
Everything in Pro, plus:
- **30 coachings per day**
- Voice coach (speak to your coach via Whisper transcription)
- Personalized weekly AI report with insights & growth metrics
- Premium guided meditations library (curated content)
- Priority support
- Early access to new features

## 📊 Cost economics (with optimizations applied)

Per-message cost: $0.0050 avg (~50% reduction from baseline via prompt caching, context compression, output cap, smart routing, FAQ cache).

Monthly margins (Apple IAP path, 15% Small Business cut + 20% VAT):
- Pro avg usage (5 msg/day): +€4.06 net margin
- Pro at cap (10 msg/day): +€3.37 net margin  
- Zen avg usage (15 msg/day): +€8.45 net margin (incl. voice/reports overhead)
- Zen at cap (30 msg/day): +€7.15 net margin

Stripe path margins are ~25% higher than Apple IAP (no 15% platform cut).

## 🎯 Conversion target
- Free → Paid: 4% target
- Among paid: 75% Pro / 25% Zen split
